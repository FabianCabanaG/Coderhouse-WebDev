What is a Random act of kindness?

Random Acts of Kindness are simple, selfless actions that individuals perform to help others without expecting anything in return.
It could be as small as a smile, a compliment, or holding the door for someone, or something bigger like donating to a charity, volunteering at a shelter, or paying for someone's meal.

The idea of performing random acts of kindness has been around for a long time, but it gained widespread popularity in the 1990s. Since then, there have been various campaigns and initiatives aimed at encouraging people to perform random acts of kindness.
